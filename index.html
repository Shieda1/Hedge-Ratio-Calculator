What is the hedge ratio calculator?
The hedge ration calculator is a tool that determines the proportion of your investment portfolio that is hedged from investment risks.
How to calculate hedge ratio?
To calculate hedge ratio you should use the following formula:
hedge ratio = (value of hedge position / value of total exposure) * 100
Why use hedge ratio calculator?
The hedge ration calculator is used in order to measure the proportion of your investment portfolio that is hedged from investment risks.
